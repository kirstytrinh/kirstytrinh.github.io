It's a beautiful day for Mexican food
Location: Rosa Mexicano
My friends and I went to Rosa Mexicano which is a Mexican restaurant in South Melbourne. We order the Chicangas, white sangria, and margaritas, and it was delicious. In the future, we will be able to check the menu and customer review of a restaurant by just standing outside of it.
