Dooboo Korean Tofu House
Dooboo Korean Tofu House may be found in Melbourne's CBD and Glen Waverly neighbourhoods. I went to the Glen Waverly location and ordered the signature soon tofu set with boneless chicken dish. This set included rice, soup, and a side dish. When we order food in the future we will know which item is what because it will be labelled in a colour box.
