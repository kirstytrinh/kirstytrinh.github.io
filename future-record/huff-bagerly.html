huff bagerly
My daily task is to grab lunch, and my favourite place to do it is Huff Bagelry, which serves sweet and savoury bagels in a lively cafe with elegant wood cladding and a takeaway window. In Melbourne, there are two stores: Carnegie and Mentone, and I visited Carnegie. Smoked salmon bagel and chicken schnitzel is what my friend and I ordered. In the future we will be able to see all of the ingredients in our food before we eat. For example, I can see that the smoked salmon bagel is a sesame bagel with smoked salmon, capers and cream cheese.
