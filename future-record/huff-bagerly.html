What's in my bagel?
Location: Huff Bagerly
My daily task is to grab lunch at Huff Bagelry, which serves sweet and savoury bagels. My friend and I ordered the smoked salmon bagel and chicken schnitzel. In the future we will be able to see all of the ingredients in our food before we eat.
