TBT Boat Cruise
Publication
I was given the task of designing a two-sided poster to promote a university social club event called TBT boat cruise. The event's theme is a return to high school, with partons dressed in their uniforms. I created the poster by combining a cruise ship and the ocean with school elements such as a chalk board, line paper, and uniform tie. The reverse of the poster contains a brochure with more information about the event and the club.
