Idenity
The Museum of Monopoly investigates the numerous, inventive monopolies that have been developed over the years. Will delve into its past and how it all started. The artist's approach to turning current pop culture into a theme for a board game. This is an interactive museum where each guest participates in a scavenger hunt to see who can find the most items. Additionally, Monopoly has distinctive characteristics that I'd like to keep and bring to the museum. The colours of the properties in Monopoly are light blue, dark blue, red, orange, pink, yellow, grey, and brown which are a significant identity of monopoly.
